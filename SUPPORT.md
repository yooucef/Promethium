# Support

## Community Support

For general questions, discussions, and community support, please use the following channels:

1.  **GitHub Issues**: For bug reports and feature requests. Please follow the templates provided.
2.  **GitHub Discussions**: For Q&A, ideas, and general discussion.

## Commercial Support

Promethium is an open-source project. If you require commercial support, custom feature development, or SLA-backed maintenance, please contact the repository maintainers directly.

## Response Times

We aim to respond to high-priority security issues within 48 hours. Community support is provided on a best-effort basis by volunteers.
